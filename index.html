📋 1. Project Title & Description

Project Title: Personal Cybersecurity Awareness Guide

This project will create a clear and accessible cybersecurity guide that teaches everyday internet users how to protect themselves from common online threats such as phishing, weak passwords, and unsafe browsing. The guide will provide step-by-step tips, examples, and best practices to promote safer digital habits. It will benefit students, professionals, and the general public by increasing awareness and reducing the risk of cyberattacks.

Personal Mission Connection:

This project supports my personal mission of helping others stay safe and confident in the digital world. It reflects my values of education, security, and empowerment by providing people with practical tools to protect themselves online.

Vision Alignment:

Strategic Goal Support:

This project advances my strategic goal of building a strong foundation in cybersecurity knowledge while also improving my project management and communication skills. 💼 3. Business Case & Justification

It will improve my portfolio with a tangible deliverable that demonstrates my ability to simplify technical concepts for diverse audiences. 📏 4. High-Level Scope

✅ In Scope for Semester Project

  • Develop a cybersecurity guide covering safe browsing, phishing awareness, and password management
  • Include practical checklists and step-by-step safety tips
  • Provide examples of common online scams and how to avoid them

🗺️ Roadmap Items (≤6 Months)

  • Add interactive elements such as short quizzes or scenarios to test user knowledge
  • Transalte the guide into other languages

❌ Out of Scope

  • Advanced technical training or coding lessons
  • Setting up cybersecurity tools or software for users
  • Turning the project into a nationwide awareness campaign.
